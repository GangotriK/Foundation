About Us

  • Responsive
  • Our Aim

Fantasy Foundation, a non-profit organization in Solapur, Maharashtra, is dedicated to social upliftment, equality, and women empowerment. Committed to transforming lives, we strive to support the neglected and marginalized, ensuring they receive opportunities for a better future. Registered under the Society Registration Act of 1860 (Reg. No. MH/581/22, dated 02-09-2022), our 11-member management team works tirelessly to bridge societal gaps and create lasting impact. With unwavering determination, we continue our mission to fight for justice, dignity, and empowerment for all

Skill India and Industry Partnership

Fantasy Foundation, in collaboration with the Ministry of Skill Development & Entrepreneurship and NSDC, bridges industry partnerships under the Skill India Mission. Through a single-window facilitation system, we connect Corporates, Foundations, Government, and Community Organizations for impactful skill development. Our NGO provides infrastructure across Maharashtra & Karnataka, offering training in Telecommunication, ITES, Healthcare, and Logistics. By equipping the youth with industry-relevant skills, we enhance their employability and open doors to meaningful careers.
